FAQ

Performance Marketing Questions, Answered

Common questions about how GroScale approaches player acquisition, trader acquisition, media buying and compliant advertising for iGaming, Forex and Crypto brands.

What does a performance marketing agency for iGaming actually do?

A performance marketing agency for iGaming plans and runs paid media, affiliate and SEO campaigns specifically for sportsbook and casino operators, with every campaign measured against first-time deposits (FTDs), cost per acquisition (CPA) and return on ad spend (ROAS) rather than clicks or impressions. GroScale manages compliance-safe ad accounts, creative testing and tracking so player acquisition campaigns scale without triggering account bans.

How is Forex broker marketing different from other paid advertising?

Forex marketing has to balance strict financial advertising regulations with the need to attract traders who actually fund and trade — not just sign up. GroScale builds verified lead generation funnels for brokers and prop-trading firms, using compliant creative and targeting to reduce wasted spend on low-intent leads and lower overall CPA.

Can GroScale help with crypto exchange and Web3 marketing?

Yes. We run global user acquisition campaigns for crypto exchanges, Web3 platforms and token launches, covering everything from KYC-verified sign-ups to liquidity-focused trader acquisition — built to hold up through volatile market cycles rather than chase short-term spikes in traffic.

What marketing channels do you use for player and trader acquisition?

GroScale runs paid campaigns across Google, Meta and native advertising, combined with affiliate marketing, technical SEO and conversion rate optimisation. Channel mix is decided by your target CPA, GEO and industry — not a one-size-fits-all media plan.

How quickly can I expect to see results from performance marketing?

Most GroScale clients see measurable movement in FTDs, leads or ROAS within the first 4–8 weeks as campaigns are optimised, with more significant scale typically achieved by month three as we expand into new GEOs and channels based on early performance data.
